Online Learning Platform

An online learning platform is an information system that provides a safe learning environment where students can take online courses. Such online learning platforms are often called ‘online course marketplaces’ because they give learners the opportunity to search for and pay for online courses directly. While some of these course marketplaces are used to feature online courses, the majority are more educational-based and strictly offer university-level education.
